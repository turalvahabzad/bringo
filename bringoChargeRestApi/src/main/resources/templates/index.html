About Us

We provide mobile charge stations for Electric Vehicles (EV)

We aim to make electric vehicle charging accessible to all, whether in urban areas, remote locations, or during road trips. Our mobile charging stations are designed to meet the diverse needs of EV users.

Our Mission

At Bringo Charge, our mission is to revolutionize the electric vehicle charging experience by providing convenient, reliable, and sustainable mobile charging solutions. We are committed to accelerating the adoption of electric vehicles and reducing the world's carbon footprint by empowering EV owners with easy access to clean energy wherever they are.

Our Solution

Bringo Charge proposes a solution to address these challenges by introducing CaaS and mobile charge stations for Electric Vehicles (EVs) in Slovakia.

Key features of our solution include:

  • Flexibility and Mobility: Bringo Charge's mobile charge stations can be deployed flexibly to meet the evolving needs of EV owners, including temporary charging events, remote locations, and areas with high demand for charging infrastructure.
  • Convenience: By bringing the charging station directly to the user, Bringo Charge eliminates the need for EV owners to travel to fixed charging stations, saving time and reducing inconvenience.
  • Accessibility: Our solution increases accessibility to EV charging infrastructure, particularly in rural and suburban areas where traditional charging stations may be scarce. This encourages broader adoption of electric mobility and supports sustainable transportation initiatives.
  • Scalability: Bringo Charge's fleet of mobile charge stations can be scaled up or down based on demand, allowing us to respond dynamically to changes in EV adoption rates, geographic trends, and seasonal variations in travel patterns.
